Frequently Asked Questions

How do I add a watermark?

1. Tap "Select Photo" to choose an image from your library
2. Enter your watermark text
3. Tap "Settings" to customize font, size, color, and opacity
4. Tap "Save" to save the watermarked photo

Where are my watermarked photos saved?

Watermarked photos are saved directly to your Photos library.

Can I use emoji as watermarks?

Yes! You can use any text or emoji as your watermark.

How do I adjust spacing between watermarks?

In Settings, enable "Fill Photo" and use the Horizontal and Vertical spacing sliders.
